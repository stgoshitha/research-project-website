AI-Based System for Identification and Quality Assessment of Sri Lankan Medicinal Plants

A smart and modern research platform designed to identify medicinal plants found in Sri Lanka and assess their quality using intelligent image-based analysis. This project blends academic research, AI innovation, and practical usability in one professional solution.
