# GreenAyu-Website

## Overview
GreenAyu-Website is a research project website developed for the presentation of our undergraduate research on the **AI-Based System for Identification and Quality Assessment of Sri Lankan Medicinal Plants**.

This website was created to showcase the research background, milestones, project documents, presentation slides, team information, demo video, and contact details in a professional and user-friendly way.

## Research Title
**AI-Based System for Identification and Quality Assessment of Sri Lankan Medicinal Plants**

## Purpose
The main purpose of this website is to provide a digital platform to present our research project in a clear and organized manner. It highlights the project domain, research objectives, methodology, used technologies, project progress, and team contributions.

## Features
- Responsive research project website
- Modern landing page with hero section
- Research domain section
- Technologies used section
- Project milestones timeline
- Documents section
- Presentation slides section
- About us / research team section
- Demo video section
- Contact section
